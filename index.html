About Me

Hi, I'm Ruchi Gurnani

With over a decade of experience in nutrition and wellness coaching, I've helped thousands of people transform their relationship with food and achieve lasting health results.

My approach is simple: no crash diets, no deprivation. I create personalized nutrition plans that work with your lifestyle, your preferences, and your goals. Whether you're managing a health condition, preparing for a special occasion, or simply want to feel your best, I'm here to guide you every step of the way.

I believe healthy eating should be enjoyable, sustainable, and suited to your Indian kitchen. That's why my plans include familiar foods, easy recipes, and the flexibility you need to succeed.
